Md Mamunur Rahaman

PhD Candidate • UNSW Sydney

Sydney, Australia

I am a researcher at the intersection of biomedical science and artificial intelligence, developing computational methods for precision healthcare. My work focuses on multimodal medical AI—integrating histopathology, radiology, molecular, and clinical data—to build robust models for diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment stratification.

Currently, I am a PhD Candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, UNSW Sydney, advised by Prof. Dr. Erik Meijering, Prof. Dr. Anant Madabhushi, and A/Prof. Dr. Ewan Millar.

I also serve as a Visiting Fellow at Georgia Tech & Emory University (USA) and a Visiting Researcher at the Schüffler Lab, TU Munich (Germany). My research has been published in top-tier venues including NeurIPS, IEEE JBHI, and Journal of Advanced Research.
